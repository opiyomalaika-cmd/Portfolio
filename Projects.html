Week 04 Podcast Review

The Problem With AI Benchmarks

This assignment focused on why AI benchmarks can be misleading. It emphasized that strong scores on controlled tests do not always show how a system behaves in live, real-world settings.

Focus: Evaluation limits, observability, and trust
AEC Relevance: Better validation of field and workflow AI tools
Theme: Real-world performance matters most

This work examined the shift from text-generating systems to more autonomous agents that can reason, plan, and complete multi-step tasks. It considered how these systems may reshape industries and organizational workflows.
